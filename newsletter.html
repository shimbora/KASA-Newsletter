Welcome to KASA @ UIUC!

KASA was founded in the 1970s with the intention of providing a community on campus for the Korean American population on campus. KASA strives to be a strong, proud, and dedicated unified student organization of excellent leaders, constantly striving to serve, improve and educate UIUC and the surrounding community about Korean-American heritage.

Year 2019-2020 Recap!

November 11th aka 11/11 is a famous modern Korean Holiday that is celebrated by giving and receiving a long thin cracker called pepero! Last year KASA made our own pepero and had a funraising event at the Quad

Rice cake is one of the dishes Koreans enjoy during their fall holidays. At KASA we made mochi with red bean fillings!

When the weather is nice outside, KASA gather up and open social events such as Korean BBQ party!

We also have indoors event such as game night, movie night, and KDrama nights!

This year most of the events will be arragned online, however, we will still have a lot of fun!
